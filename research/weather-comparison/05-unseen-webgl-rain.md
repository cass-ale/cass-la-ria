# Unseen Studio WebGL Rain (Ash Thornton)
Source: https://unseen.co/labs/webgl-rain/

## Observations
- Award-winning creative studio (Awwwards recognized)
- Uses WebGL (heavy GPU rendering)
- Has "Click & Hold to slow time" interaction — time dilation effect
- Has "Options" panel for customization
- Loading bar suggests significant asset loading (textures, shaders)
- Black background with white rain streaks — high contrast approach
- The page didn't fully render in headless browser (WebGL-dependent)

## Key Concept: Time Dilation
The "slow time" interaction is a powerful UX concept:
- Holding click slows all rain to a crawl
- Creates a meditative, contemplative feel
- Gives users a sense of control over the environment
- Could be adapted for our system (already have cursor interaction)

## Applicability
- Time dilation is a premium interaction concept
- High contrast (white on black) is a design choice, not applicable to our pink palette
- The Options panel concept could inspire customization
- WebGL techniques not directly portable to our 2D canvas system
